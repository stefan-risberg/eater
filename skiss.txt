Food items
    id - unique id
    name - Name of product
    type - type of food, "tags"
    calorie - calorie amount for 100g in kcal
    protein - protein amount for 100g in g
    carbohydrate - carbs amount for 100g in g
    fat - fats amount for 100g in g

Recepies
    id - unique id
    name - name of recepie
    foods - list of food ids
    amount - list of last used amount in g
    calorie - total amount of calories
    protein - total amount of proteins
    carbohydrate - total amount of carbohydrates
    fat - total amount of fats

Eaten
    Food Item
        id - id of item eaten.
        amount - amount of eaten food item.

    Recepie
        id - id of recepie.
        foods - list of foods eaten.
        amounts - list of amount of foods.
